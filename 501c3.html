Helping 501(c)(3)s Grow Online

I work with nonprofit organizations to design, manage, and grow their online presence through professional websites and meaningful graphics that inspire action and build community.

What I Do

Website Design & Management

I help nonprofits build and maintain modern, user-friendly websites that clearly share their mission and make it easy for people to get involved. From hosting setup to ongoing updates, I handle it all.

Graphic Design

From event flyers and posters to digital assets for social media, I create visuals that bring your message to life — clean, impactful, and aligned with your organization’s brand.

Technical Support

I provide easy-to-understand tech support for nonprofits, ensuring their websites, email systems, and online tools run smoothly and securely without the stress of managing it alone.

Collaboration & Consultation

I work directly with 501(c)(3) teams to understand their needs, offer digital guidance, and create practical solutions that fit their goals and budget.

Empowering Change Through Design

Every nonprofit deserves a strong, functional digital presence. Through Acole Dev, I help 501(c)(3) organizations save time, stay organized, and look professional — so they can focus on what really matters: making a difference.
